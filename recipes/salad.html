Rainbow salad with haloumi

If you are looking for a salad packed with goodness, and full of delicious and colourful ingredients (and there is a saying that the more colourful your meal, the healthier it is) then this is a salad for you. Topped with crispy-on-the-outside, oozy-on-the-inside haloumi it stands on its own as a vegetarian lunch, or a wonderful accompaniment to some grilled lamb or chicken. The dressing is quite sharp and tangy to contrast with the saltiness of the haloumi and adds zing and a little bit of heat to the whole salad.

Ingredients: (Serves 4-6):

  • 120 g mixed salad leaves
  • 1 each of medium-sized red, yellow and green capsicums
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 medium-sized fresh beetroot
  • 250 g mixed cherry tomatoes
  • 2 cobs fresh corn
  • handful fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 1 small bunch chives
  • 1/3 cup pepitas
  • 1/2 cup pecan nuts
  • 180 -200 g block of haloumi
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • juice of 1 large lemon
  • 2 teaspoons capers
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • sea salt and black pepper

Cooking instruction:

  1. Heat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Place the pepitas and pecans on a baking tray and bake for 10 minutes until toasty and crunchy.
  3. Remove the nuts from the oven and set them aside to cool.
  4. Dice the capsicums into 1 cm square pieces.
  5. Peel and julienne the carrots.
  6. Peel and julienne the beetroot, keeping it separate from other ingredients to prevent staining.
  7. Cut the tomatoes into halves.
  8. Cut the kernels off the corn cobs using a sharp knife.
  9. Rough chop the parsley and snip the chives.
  10. Prepare the dressing by placing 2 1/2 tablespoons of olive oil, the lemon juice, capers, and chilli flakes in a small glass jar with a lid, shaking well, and seasoning with sea salt and black pepper.
  11. Toss all the salad ingredients together and transfer them to a serving dish when ready to serve.
  12. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add the remaining olive oil.
  13. Cut the haloumi into 8 pieces and pan-fry until golden.
  14. Place the fried haloumi on top of the salad, pour the dressing over everything, and serve immediately.
